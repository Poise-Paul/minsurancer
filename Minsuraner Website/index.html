About Us

Minsurancer is a pioneer in modern sales of auto spare parts, Repairs and Emergency Roadside Services (ERS) in Nigeria. Offering a variety of services that impacts the quality of life of Nigerians, our ERS includes Quick Roadside Auto Repair, Fuel Delivery, Battery Jumpstart, Flat Tire Service, Lockout and Locksmith Services and Towing. Our Garage Auto Repairs take place in safe and secure premises where fitting of original spares, glass and accessories are professionally done.
